Paper accepted for ECCV 2022

The paper "WISE: Whitebox Image Stylization by Example-based Learning" authored by Winfried Lötzsch*, Max Reimann*, Martin Büßemeyer, Amir Semmo, Jürgen Döllner and Matthias Trapp (*denotes equal contribution) was accepted for publication on the European Conference for Computer Vision (ECCV 2022). It will be presented on the 23-27 Oct in Tel Aviv, Israel.
